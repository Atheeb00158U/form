How to register?

Registration Fee: Please note that registration fee is required to proceed with the registration process.
Check your email: Look for an email from our CPS Prime MUN in your inbox.
Open the email: Find the Google Forms link provided in the email.
Access the form: Click on the link to access the registration form.
Complete the form: Fill out all the required fields accurately and provide any additional information if needed.
Confirmation email: After submitting the form, you will receive an email with your committee allocation and further instructions.
